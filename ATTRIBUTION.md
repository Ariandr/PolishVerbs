# Attribution

PolishVerbs combines public lexical/frequency sources with project-specific enrichment, validation, cleanup, translations, examples, and UI code.

## KWJP Frequency Lists

Verb frequency order and frequency metadata are based on KWJP frequency lists for the balanced Corpus of Contemporary Polish.

- Website: https://kwjp.pl/lists/doc/about/en
- Repository: https://github.com/ipipan/kwjp100-varia
- Copyright notice shown by KWJP: Institute of Computer Science PAS, 2023

KWJP notes that the frequency lists are automatically compiled and may contain errors due to automatic lemmatization and morphosyntactic tagging.
